A story about us.

Tejasvi is the social and cultural festival of Marian Engineering College, Thiruvananthapuram; a pioneer in the elite institutions. It marks 2 days of absolute ecstasy, providing the budding artists a competing platform in diverse fields such as music, dance, theatre, photography, literature, fine arts, quizzing and debating. Held every 2 years it as always here this year to unravel the heaven onto the earth.
