Rules

A minimum of 6 participants & Maximum of 20 are allowed per team. In addition if present there should be one person in charge for the music and one for lights

The event should be a dance performance mixed with humour that brings out a particular theme or message

Music tracks be mp4 format & should be handed over to the event coordinator prior to the event ,at the earliest.

No voice over/dubbing allowed, but the music track can include movie dialogues.

Points shall be awarded on the basis of humour, ideas and creativity, theme/message,coordination, costume and props.

NO vulgarities will be tolerated, in the performance or costume. The event heads have the right to interfere and take action if found.

The team of participants must be currently students of the same college.

Time limit 10 mins max ( Negative marks will be allotted for teams exceeding the time limit)

The co-ordinators have the full right to interrupt the performance at any time in case of violation of the above rules.

Any team violating the above rules is subjected to direct elimination.

Spot Registration will NOT be allowed.

College ID card is mandatory at the time of reporting.

Decision of the judges will be final and no further appealing will be allowed.
